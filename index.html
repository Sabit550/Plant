📋 Key Agriculture Rules
১৯৭১
Pesticide Ordinance 1971
Regulates import, formulation, and sale of pesticides. All pesticides must be registered and used according to label instructions only.
১৯৭৭
Seeds Ordinance 1977
Controls seed quality and certification. Farmers must use certified seeds from recognized sources for guaranteed crop yield.
২০১৮
National Agriculture Policy 2018
Guides sustainable agricultural development. Includes fertilizer subsidy, irrigation support, and crop insurance for registered farmers.
SCA
Seed Certification Agency (SCA)
Certifies seeds meeting quality standards. Look for the SCA certification tag when buying seeds to ensure authenticity.
DAE
Extension Services – DAE
Department of Agricultural Extension provides free field-level advisory. Farmers can visit local Upazila Agriculture Officer for guidance.
